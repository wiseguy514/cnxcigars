The Lounge

The Room
Itself

"A cigar asks for two things the modern world rarely offers: stable air and unhurried time. We built the room around both."

Above Nimman, behind a single unmarked door, sits a room engineered for one purpose. The CNX Cigars lounge holds steady where the climate outside does not, an open floor that seats thirty in comfort, and a private reserve held back for those who ask for it.

Reserve a Seat

The Open Floor

Built for Thirty,
Calibrated for One

The main lounge is open, generous, and unhurried. Thirty-plus seats are arranged not in rows but in conversation, deep leather, low tables, and the kind of spacing that lets a room breathe. No queue, no counter, no rush toward the next guest.

This is the social heart of the Chiang Mai cigar lounge. Expatriates, travellers, and local connoisseurs share the same air here, and the room is deliberately sized so that a full house never feels like a crowd. Every cigar in the room is drawn from the same humidor.

Every cigar in the room is kept at a steady 68 to 70 percent humidity and a cool, even temperature, held there around the clock. This is the whole premise. A cigar left in the wrong conditions is a cigar already spoiled, so the humidor is controlled and monitored every day.

That control runs independently of the weather outside, kept clear of the heat and the monsoon swings of Northern Thailand. What reaches your hand has been stored properly from the day it arrived to the moment it is cut.

Get the humidity and temperature right, and everything else follows.

The Private
Allocation Reserve

Held apart from the main floor is a smaller room, reserved for parties of four to six. It is booked, not walked into. The protocol exists to protect the evening, not to gatekeep it.

On Booking

The reserve is confirmed through LINE or WhatsApp. A short message lets us prepare the room, the pairings, and the selection before you arrive. Nothing is left to chance.

On Privacy

Once the reserve is yours for the evening, it is yours alone. No shared tables, no passing traffic. This is the room for a milestone, a closed conversation, or a host who wants the night to feel considered.

On Curation

Tell us what you reach for, or tell us nothing and let Amir map the palate of the table. Either way, the selection waiting for you is chosen, not stocked.

We do not take public form submissions, and we do not list a checkout. Every visit, whether to the open floor or the private reserve, begins with a direct message. It is a deliberate friction, and it is the right one.

A message lets us hold the right seats, brief the room, and ensure that the cigars matched to your evening are in condition before you cross the door. It is the difference between arriving as a name on a list and arriving as a guest who was expected.

Lounge Questions

Practical answers for first-time visitors and regular guests alike.

Get in Touch

CNX Cigars is open daily from 12 noon to midnight. No booking is required to visit during these hours. Walk in any time and the team will look after you from the moment you arrive.

A purchase is expected if you are using the lounge space, but there is no minimum spend and no pressure. If you want to come in, look around, ask questions, and leave without buying anything on your first visit, that is fine. The lounge operates on the understanding that trust is built over time. Most first-time visitors who come in just to look end up lighting something within the hour.

The lounge uses multi-stage HEPA filtration and carbon-scrubbing intake chambers that complete a full air volume replacement every few minutes. The result is a clean, neutral atmosphere without residual smoke build-up. Guests can spend several hours in the lounge without the sensory fatigue common in poorly ventilated smoking venues. The system runs continuously during all opening hours.
