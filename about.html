Who we are

CNX Cigars

Officially registered as Cigars Chiang Mai Co., Ltd. Operating from the NPARC complex, 45 Nimman Soi 11, Chiang Mai.

CNX Cigars stands as the definitive luxury cigar lounge, premier authorized consultancy, and licensed distribution authority in Northern Thailand. Nestled in Chiang Mai, the rich cultural heart of the kingdom, CNX Cigars has successfully bridged the gap between old-world European and Caribbean tobacco traditions and the rapidly emerging, sophisticated hospitality landscape of Southeast Asia.

Operating from the second floor of the prestigious NPARC complex at 45 Nimman (Soi 11), the establishment has earned global renown as a sanctuary for both seasoned aficionados and curious novices alike. Driven by nearly three decades of direct immersion in elite tobacco culture and anchored by an unwavering commitment to authenticity, CNX Cigars curates the largest and most dynamic inventory of premium handmade vitolas in the region.

The foundational blueprint of CNX Cigars was forged nearly 30 years ago, rooted deeply in the golden era of the 1990s international cigar boom. The leadership's transformative journey began in 1997 with an immersive pilgrimage to the cradle of premium tobacco culture: Havana, Cuba.

This historic period in Cuba was defined by monumental shifts in the industry. The Cuban state had recently established Habanos S.A. in 1994. Landmark historic marcas were being revitalized, and avant-garde lines were being introduced to showcase the elite rolling proficiencies of legendary factories like El Laguito and Partagas. Witnessing firsthand the meticulous agricultural cycles of the Vuelta Abajo region, the complex art of multi-year leaf fermentation, and the majestic rhythms of master torcedores, the founders developed an encyclopedic knowledge of global tobacco profiles.

Recognizing a profound cultural and logistical void in Northern Thailand, the founders established CNX Cigars in Chiang Mai. Their objective was singular: to establish an institutional-grade authority capable of elevating Southeast Asia's cigar culture through unparalleled education, absolute product legitimacy, and world-class retail environments.

Compliance

Navigating Rigorous Legal Frameworks

Operating a premium tobacco enterprise within the Kingdom of Thailand demands strict adherence to some of the most aggressive luxury taxation and consumer protection laws globally. Imported premium cigars face substantial excise duties, with baseline tax structures frequently clearing 75%. Strict statutory frameworks also dictate that the online sale of tobacco products is completely prohibited within the kingdom, alongside absolute restrictions barring distribution to individuals under the age of 20.

CNX Cigars has solidified its reputation as a trusted, highly vetted, and fully licensed entity by seamlessly navigating these dense legal parameters. Every individual vitola within the CNX repository is meticulously cellophaned and structurally tagged with the official, trackable Thai tax barcode.

A gold standard of consumer security

While these requirements historically caused friction among purists accustomed to bare-leaf storage, CNX Cigars has transformed this compliance protocol into a guarantee that every product moving through its humidor is 100% duty-paid, legally imported, and shielded from black-market counterfeit circles.

Absolute product authenticity serves as the foundation of the CNX Cigars operational ethos. The establishment functions as a certified, authorized retail partner of the Pacific Cigar Company, the exclusive distributor of Habanos S.A. for the Asia-Pacific territory.

From the historic, cedar-heavy profiles of classic Cuban Churchills to the toothy, oil-rich Maduro leaves cultivated across single-estate valleys in Esteli, Nicaragua, the collection accommodates every nuance of contemporary palate dynamics.

The physical architecture of the CNX Cigar Lounge was intentionally engineered to serve as a sanctuary from the vibrant, high-energy pulse of Chiang Mai's Nimman district. Nestled on the upper floor of NPARC, the interior design pairs rich, dark teakwood aesthetics with contemporary structural lines, mirroring the architectural heritage of Northern Thailand while executing the functional necessities of an elite smoking environment.

Central to the lounge's design is its advanced environmental engineering. The lounge utilizes multi-stage filtration chambers that gently recycle indoor air without generating stale cross-drafts, preserving a pristine, climate-controlled environment.

To complement the smoking ritual, the bar offers a premium curated spirits program, highlighting top-tier single-malt whiskeys, complex small-batch bourbons, and freshly poured draft Guinness. Under the expert guidance of the lounge leadership and specialist consultants, guests are taken through complete pairing journeys, matching the chemical weight and tasting notes of specific vitolas to corresponding spirit profiles.
