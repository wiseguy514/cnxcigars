Tobacco is a living material. Minor fluctuations in ambient moisture destroy essential oils, alter burn characteristics, and compromise the structural integrity of the wrapper. The CNX humidor is maintained year-round using an automated, industrial-grade humidification system that introduces controlled vapor without causing contraction or expansion in the leaf.

A dedicated temperature array ensures the space never crosses critical thermal boundaries, neutralising the risk of tobacco beetle incubation. Every cigar is stored and presented exactly as it should be, from the moment it arrives to the moment it reaches your hand.

The lounge itself uses continuous multi-stage HEPA filtration and carbon-scrubbing intake chambers, executing a complete air volume replacement every several minutes. The result is a clean atmosphere where guests can smoke for hours without residual sensory fatigue.
