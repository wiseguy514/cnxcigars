The founder

Amir Nadimi

"There is a fundamental difference between a purveyor and an authority. To be a purveyor is to manage inventory. To be an authority is to curate an experience, preserve a culture, and pass down the nuanced art of the leaf."

Amir Nadimi founded CNX Cigars on thirty years with the leaf, and on a single, clear idea: that the best cigar is the one shared. He built this lounge as a community, not a shop. A confluence point for expatriates, travellers, and local connoisseurs who understand that slowing down to smoke a fine cigar is an act of mindfulness.

He speaks the language of draw, construction, and flavour evolution with the precision of a craftsman. Whether you are exploring your first cigar or chasing a rare vitola, the knowledge on offer here is real and freely shared.

Amir arrived in Chiang Mai in 2022 and found what many had overlooked: no dedicated space for cigar culture in Northern Thailand. He began organising gatherings, building a community from the ground up. In May 2024, CNX Cigars opened its doors on Nimmana Haeminda Road and became the region's premier international cigar lounge.
